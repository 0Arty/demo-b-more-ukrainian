About us. This festival is established as the way not only to promote Ukrainian culture, but also to say Thank You to the people of the United States for continuous support of Ukraine in its fight for independence.

This is a family oriented event with live music, arts and crafts vendors, Ukrainian cuisine and good time for all. Visit us at the Baltimore Penninsula as we kick off the inaugural festivities on June 7 & 8, 2026!
